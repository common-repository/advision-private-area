Manage private contents for your customers, sellers, employer or everyone you need. Upload documents, notices or other files to your audience.

== Description ==

This simple plugin allow you manage easily your audience like Customers, Sellers, Employers or anyone else.
You can share files like documents, bills, notices or other to each person you need. They will get a private area where they will found anything you share with their.

<b>How to use this plugin:</b>
- Choose the page where you want to display the private area of each customer and just copy and paste this shortcode <b>[adv_reserved_area]</b>
- Refresh your permalinks
- After you install the plugin a new user role <b>"Site Manager"</b> will create. You can assign it to each user who should be add new content to your audience.
- You can also easily manage the role capabilities by other plugins like "User role manager".
- All users with <b>subscriber</b> role can have access to their private area.
- The whole plugin is wpml compatible. This consento to you an easy and fast translation in your language.
